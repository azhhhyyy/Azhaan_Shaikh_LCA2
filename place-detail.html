Description

Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification in the city of Pune. Built in 1732, it was the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire until 1818. The fort itself is a major tourist attraction and one of the most popular historical places in Maharashtra. The massive palace-fort, built in 1732, was once the heart of the Maratha power.

How To Access

Shaniwar Wada is easily accessible by public transport, and if needed, but there is a lack of parking availability for 4-wheelers. Due to the small roads around the palace fort, we recommend traveling on a 2-wheeler or nearby use of public transport.
